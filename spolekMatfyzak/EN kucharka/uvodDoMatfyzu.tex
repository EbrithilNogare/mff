\section{Basic information about Matfyz}
The Faculty of Mathematics and Physics is one of seventeen faculties of the
Charles University and it is, of course, by far the best one according to our
opinion.
One Matfyz "legend" says that the faculty was established in 1952 when Vojtěch
Jarník (famous Czech mathematician, namesake of Jarník's algorithm for minimum
spanning trees, worked in number theory, mathematical analysis, and graph
algorithms) succeeded, using mathematical induction, in proving that Lenin
evolved from monkeys and was expelled from the Faculty of Science at Charles
University. Exasperated, he climbed the Albertov stairs, sat down on the grass
and then wrote down all of his differential and integral calculus.
The Dean's departments was later built on this "sacred" place for Matfyz.
Nowadays it is possible to find the faculty buildings in almost half of the
city.
Almost 3000 students (including PhD students), of which approximately 500 are in
their first year, are educated with the (sometimes even overly) enthusiastic
help of more than 500 teachers with various academic degrees. Every fourth
student is a woman.
The students travel among four locations in which our faculty is based (Karlín,
Karlov, Malá Strana, Troja), Charles University Sports Center (SCUK) in
Hostivař, dormitories and refectories.
Each of the buildings has its characteristic name, address and way of naming its
lecture rooms.

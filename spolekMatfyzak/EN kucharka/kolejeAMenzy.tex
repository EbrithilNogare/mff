\section{Dormitories and refectories of the UK}
You will probably also want to stay and eat somewhere while studying in Prague. Most of the students from Matfyz stay at the student hall of residence 17. listopadu (so-called Troja), at least for a few years. 
Therefore most of this chapter will focus on it but it does not matter that you could not choose to stay anywhere else, lots of the students of Matfyz also stay at the other dormitories or in a private accommodation.
